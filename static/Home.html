About API ALERT


Imagine you are the head of a class and you need to send out a reminder of an upcoming event. Ergo, you go down the route of emailing your high school students regarding the update. Next day, you come to school and all the students say they never checked their email! Api alert is the solution to this problem.


API Alert is an application that takes in the type of communication someone wants to recieve alerts from, groups them into 'class', then saves the personalized data. Therefore, when you type an alert, the preferences are taken into an account, and whoever is in the group will recieve an alert from whatever they chose as their API.
